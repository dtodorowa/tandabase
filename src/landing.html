How to use tandabase

01.

Explore or Import

Browse sets built by the community. Want to make your own? Drop in an Apple Music XML or CSV. The app auto-detects tandas and finds the YouTube links for you.

02.

Active Listening

Press play. When the song ends, it stops. Take a breath, check the orchestra, note the year, and manually click next. You're here to study, not to hit shuffle and walk away.

03.

Fix Glitches

The search function is still under development. If a track pulls up a wrong recording, hit "Wrong video?" to swap it out and help us fix things as a community.
